What is Ask The Alumni?

Ask The Alumni (ATA) is a student-driven initiative that connects IIT (BHU) first-years with alumni to help them navigate college life. From academics to hostel life, fun experiences, and valuable lessons—ATA offers a unique opportunity to gain insights and guidance directly from those who have walked the same path before you.

Benefits of Participation

Meaningful Connection

Connect with successful alumni and build lasting professional relationships

Industry Insights

Gain practical knowledge about your field from those who've walked the path

Career and Life Guidance

Receive personalized advice on career choices and opportunities
